Customer Support

A ticketing system organizes and tracks customer inquiries, complaints, or requests in a single, manageable queue. Each customer interaction is assigned a unique ticket ID, making it easy for support agents to follow up, prioritize, and resolve issues systematically. This system also enables tracking of ticket status (e.g., open, pending, resolved), which helps support teams monitor progress and improve response times. Additionally, it provides valuable data for analyzing common customer issues, agent performance, and areas for improvement.
